Find a Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code

This code gives Companies House a description of your company’s nature of business.

You should use the SIC code that is the closest match to your business activity when you register your company. You can use up to 4 codes.

See the full list of SIC codes or read the Guidance for SIC Codes.
