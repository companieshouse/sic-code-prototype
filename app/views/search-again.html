This code provides Companies House with a description of your company's nature of business.

Find the SIC code(s) that is the closest match to your business activity, it does not have to be an exact match.

To search for a SIC code you should:

  • Describe the goods or services the business produces or sells, for example ‘clothing retailing’ not ‘retailing’.
  • If you are self-employed, describe what you do, for example ‘dairy farming’ not ‘dairy farmer’.
  • Check that the keywords are spelt correctly.
  • Try different keywords or variations.
